Paragraph

If you have or plan to compose lecture notes electronically, you might consider authoring them in PreTeXt, much as this document is. There is a small investment in getting up and running, but once going, it is fairly trivial to produce nice-looking HTML and/or PDF (via LaTeX) versions 1 . See the PreTeXt project page and particularly the pretext-support Google group, which is full of generous folks who can help you start from scratch or convert existing materials.
